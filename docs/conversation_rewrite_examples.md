# 英会話書換例集

要修正・全面改稿の代表例と、自然な米語への書換例です。初級1000語レベルを前提としています。

---

## 1. 銀行口座開設

### Before（要修正）
**Speaker B:** We establish your user name from the form you filled yesterday. The direction is to create a password. I reveal the requirements onto the screen. Eight chars. Number and letter.

**問題**: establish / direction / reveal の誤用、短文羅列

### After
**Speaker B:** We'll set up your username from the form you filled out yesterday. Next, create a password. The requirements are shown on the screen—eight characters, with both letters and numbers.

---

## 2. 病院

### Before（全面改稿）
**Speaker A:** Got it. Production. I will watch the weight of symptoms. A bit careful. I perform rest. Thank you, Doctor.

**問題**: Production 文脈外、weight of symptoms 直訳、perform rest 不自然

### After
**Speaker A:** Got it. I'll keep an eye on my symptoms and get plenty of rest. Thank you, Doctor.

---

## 3. オンラインバンキング

### Before（要修正）
**Speaker A:** I want to establish online access. Direction from here? Can you reveal the steps? I need to get onto the app fast.

**問題**: establish / direction / reveal の誤用

### After
**Speaker A:** I'd like to set up online access. What do I do next? Can you walk me through the steps? I need to get on the app quickly.

---

## 4. 税関・郵便

### Before（全面改稿）
**Speaker B:** Yes. Declare. Value. Contain. Honest. Amount. Peace. No. Trouble. Cultural. Gifts. Usually. Low. Value. OK. Employee. Helps. Fill.

**問題**: 単語羅列のみ、文として未成立

### After
**Speaker B:** Yes. Please declare what's inside and the value. Be honest about the amount. For things like gifts, the value is usually low, so it's no problem. Our staff can help you fill out the form.

---

## 5. ビジネスプレゼン

### Before（要修正）
**Speaker A:** Good afternoon. Today we present. Market study. Our executive team. Set the goals. Various segments. We will cover. Rather comprehensive.

**問題**: 短文羅列、文として不自然

### After
**Speaker A:** Good afternoon. Today we'll present our market study. Our executive team has set clear goals, and we'll cover various segments. It's a comprehensive overview.

---

## 6. カスタム・コンサル

### Before（要修正）
**Speaker B:** We establish tonight. We treat it as the first session. The trip begins. Evening 7 PM. We establish the link. I treat and email it now.

**問題**: establish / treat / trip / evening の誤用と羅列

### After
**Speaker B:** We'll start tonight. Let's treat this as our first session. The journey begins at 7 PM. I'll set up the link and email it to you now.

---

## 7. 道案内

### Before（要修正）
**Speaker B:** Central. Easy. Red line. From here. Three stops. You cannot miss it. Big station. Authority hub. Main design. Clear.

**問題**: 単語羅列、口語として不自然

### After
**Speaker B:** It's easy. Take the Red Line from here. Three stops. You can't miss it—it's a big station and a main hub.

---

## 8. ショッピング

### Before
**Speaker A:** Environmental. Good. My sister cares. Present for myself. Treat. What size? I am 42. European. Not sure US.

**問題**: 単語羅列

### After
**Speaker A:** I'm looking for something eco-friendly. It's a gift for my sister—she cares about that. What size do you have? I'm a 42 European. I'm not sure about US sizes.

---

## 書換時の共通ルール

1. **1発話1〜2文**: 長くなりすぎない
2. **頻出チャンクを優先**: "I'd like to ~", "Can you ~", "Let me ~", "We'll ~"
3. **語彙は結果的に**: 目標語彙を無理に詰め込まない
4. **日本語訳は維持**: 意図を変えない
